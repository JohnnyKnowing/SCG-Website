Task 01

Washington State Licensure & Supervision

If you have recently graduated from your master's program, your first immediate milestone is applying for your Associate credential (e.g., LMHCA) with the Washington State Department of Health (DOH). This allows you to practice under approved clinical supervision.

Supervisor Information for Your Application

To establish Johnny Spangler, LMHC as your approved clinical supervisor, you will need his registration details:

  • Supervisor Name: Johnny Spangler, LMHC
  • License Status: Licensed Mental Health Counselor (WA State)
  • Credential Number: LH60888998
  • Contact Email: johnny@spangler.care

Step-by-Step Licensure Guide

1

Access the WA DOH Provider Portal

Visit the Washington State Department of Health Licensing System and create an account in the Provider Portal.

WA DOH Provider Portal Login →

2

Initiate the Associate Application

Select "Apply for a New Credential" and select your specific path (usually Mental Health Counselor Associate or Marriage and Family Therapist Associate). Pay the state credentialing application fee.

3

Submit the Supervisor Agreement Form

You must fill out and submit the official DOH Supervision form. Download the form, fill out your candidate details, and email it to Johnny at johnny@spangler.care so he can sign the supervisor section and return it to the DOH.

Task 02

Applying for your NPI-1 Number

The National Provider Identifier (NPI) is a unique 10-digit identification number issued by the federal government (CMS) to healthcare providers. As an individual therapist, you need an NPI Type 1 (Individual) number. This number stays with you throughout your entire career and is required by HIPAA for all insurance billing, records, and credentialing systems.

Taxonomy Code for Mental Health Associates

During the application, you will be asked to select your Provider Taxonomy. For most graduates entering associate counseling, the standard taxonomy code is 101YM0800X (Mental Health Counselor) or 106H00000X (Marriage & Family Therapist).

Step-by-Step NPI-1 Guide

1

Create an NPPES Account

Visit the National Plan and Provider Enumeration System (NPPES) website. Create an I&A (Identity & Access) account to login.

Go to the NPPES Portal →

2

Apply for an Individual NPI

Select "Apply for an NPI for myself (Type 1 Individual Provider)". Follow the application pages: enter your legal name, social security number, contact details, and graduation/practice location.

3

Enter Practice Information & Taxonomy

Use your residential or private office address for mailing details. Select the appropriate Counselor/Therapist taxonomy code and link it to your pending or active Washington state license.

4

Receive & Save Your NPI-1

Once submitted, CMS usually issues your 10-digit NPI via email within 24 to 48 hours. Keep this email safe, as you will need to input the NPI on Task 4.

Task 03 • FUTURE REFERENCE ONLY

Setting Up Your CAQH ProView Profile Not Required Yet

Important Note: Not Required for Onboarding Right Now

Spangler Counseling Group is not currently set up to accept commercial insurance. Consequently, registering with CAQH is not required to start your employment and is provided here purely for future reference. You do not need to perform these steps today.

When the practice expands to accept insurance panels in the future, CAQH (Council for Affordable Quality Healthcare) will be used as our secure online database for credentialing. It allows you to enter your background, transcripts, licenses, and references once, which insurance carriers then use to verify your credentials.

Why CAQH Will Be Used in the Future

When we transition to an insurance model, we will use your CAQH profile to add you to panels (such as Premera, Regence, Kaiser, and Optum). We are keeping this guide here so you have a seamless, step-by-step walkthrough ready to go when that day comes.

Future Reference: Step-by-Step CAQH Setup

1

Register for a CAQH ProView Account

Register as a new provider on the CAQH ProView portal. You will need your active State License Number or your newly received NPI-1 to initialize registration.

Go to CAQH ProView Portal →

2

Complete the Core Profile Modules

Fill out all biographical details, clinical education history, active licenses, insurance certifications, and CV details. This is detailed and can take up to an hour.

3

Upload Required Documents

Upload clean PDF copies of your graduation diploma/transcripts, Washington State associate counselor license certificate, professional liability insurance certificate, and a signed W9 form.

4

Authorize Spangler Counseling Group

Navigate to the "Authorize" tab within your CAQH dashboard. Make sure you set permissions to **Global Authorization** or specifically search and authorize **Spangler Counseling Group** so we can access your credentials for paneling.
